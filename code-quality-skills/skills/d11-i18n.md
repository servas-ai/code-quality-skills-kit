## AUDIT

- **Hardcoded German strings in JSX**: `rg -n ">[A-ZÄÖÜ][\p{L} ]{4,}<" src/` (matches inline German title-case text).
- **Hardcoded labels in attributes**: `rg -n "(placeholder|title|aria-label)=\"[A-ZÄÖÜ][^\"]+\"" src/` — flag any not pulled from a translation table.
